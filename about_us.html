Our Story

In the heart of a bustling city, Rohit, Riddhi, and Adithi discovered a shared passion for gardening amidst the concrete landscape. Starting with a humble rooftop garden, they transformed a small urban space into a thriving oasis of greenery. As their garden flourished, so did their desire to share their knowledge and passion with others.

Driven by their belief that everyone should have the opportunity to experience the joy of gardening, Rohit, Riddhi, and Adithi founded Home Gardening. Their mission: to simplify the art of gardening and make it accessible to urban dwellers and beginners alike. Through practical advice, expert guidance, and curated products, Home Gardening has grown into a trusted resource for garden enthusiasts around the world.

Today, Home Gardening continues to nurture a community of like-minded individuals who share a love for plants and a commitment to sustainable living. Rohit, Riddhi, and Adithi's journey from rooftop gardeners to founders of a global gardening community reflects their dedication to fostering a greener, more connected world through the simple act of gardening.

Our legal basis for collecting data

We will only use your personal data if we have a legal basis to do so. This means that we will only use personal data where we have your explicit consent, where it is necessary in order to perform a contract with you, to comply with a legal duty or right, to protect your vital interests, or where the processing of your personal information is in our legitimate interest and not overridden by your legal rights.

There are generally three legal bases under which we may process your data:

  • Consent: When consent is required we will always ask for your explicit consent to processing your personal data.
  • Contract: When you make a purchase from us or become a member, you are entering a contract with us. We need to process and store your data in order to perform this contract.
  • Legitimate interests: In some situations we collect and process your personal data for purposes that are in our legitimate organisational interests. In doing so we consider that there is no overriding prejudice to you and that you would reasonably expect us to use your data in this way.
